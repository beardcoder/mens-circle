## Voice vs Tone

**Voice** is constant — it's your brand's personality in words. It doesn't change.

**Tone** adapts to context — celebratory for success states, empathetic for errors, clear for instructions. Same voice, different emotional register.

## Archetype Voice Examples

### Modern/Bold (Digital Studio)

**Headline:** "We build what others can't."
**Subheadline:** "Brand, digital, and AI for firms that lead."
**CTA:** "Start a project" (not "Get in touch" — too passive)
**Error:** "Something broke. Try again." (direct, no sugar-coating)

### Luxury/Editorial (Fragrance)

**Headline:** "Discover your signature."
**Subheadline:** "Curated fragrances inspired by the world's finest houses."
**CTA:** "Explore the collection" (not "Shop now" — too transactional)
**Error:** "We couldn't complete your request. Please try again." (polished)

### Warm/Inclusive (Mental Health Clinic)

**Headline:** "Your space to heal."
**Subheadline:** "Find the right therapist for your journey."
**CTA:** "Take the quiz" or "Find your match" (non-threatening, inviting)
**Error:** "Something went wrong on our end. We're working on it." (takes blame)

### Authoritative (Legal)

**Headline:** "Aggressive defense. Real results."
**Subheadline:** "Criminal defense for serious charges."
**CTA:** "Book a consultation" (professional, action-oriented)
**Error:** "Unable to process. Contact us directly at [phone]." (provides alternative immediately)

### Professional (Healthcare)

**Headline:** "Your neighbourhood pharmacy, reimagined."
**Subheadline:** "Trusted care, modern convenience."
**CTA:** "View our services" (informational)
**Error:** "We hit a snag. Please refresh or call us." (friendly, helpful)

## Microcopy Patterns

### CTAs by Brand Personality

| Context | Bold | Luxury | Warm | Authoritative | Professional |
|---------|------|--------|------|--------------|-------------|
| Primary action | "Start a project" | "Explore" | "Get started" | "Book now" | "Learn more" |
| Secondary | "View work" | "Discover more" | "How it works" | "View results" | "Contact us" |
| Newsletter | "Stay sharp" | "Join our world" | "Stay connected" | "Get updates" | "Subscribe" |

### Empty States

- **Bold**: "Nothing here yet. Let's change that."
- **Luxury**: "Your collection awaits."
- **Warm**: "No worries — let's find what you're looking for."
- **Authoritative**: "No results found."
- **Professional**: "No items to display."
